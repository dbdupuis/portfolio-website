My Story

I’ve always loved pretty things and figuring out how to make things better. When I discovered Photoshop CS5 in high school, you couldn’t keep me off my computer. I was always working on some kind of project. At Clemson, I discovered Graphic Communications. A whole program dedicated to problem-solving your way to get the print in your hand to match the design on the screen? I was hooked!

My professional career started in event marketing but quickly transitioned to include digital marketing as well. In my early days, I had so much fun hopping from field to field and learning what I liked and disliked about each. Eventually, I found myself getting stagnant, and a friend was telling me about her job as a UX Researcher. Now you’re telling me I can solve problems in an aesthetically pleasing way?

As I was diving head-first into UX, I realized many of these principles are transferable to marketing and started refining both skills. And that brings us to today! I currently work full-time in Growth Marketing focusing on re-engagement and work on UX projects part-time as a volunteer or freelancer. In my downtime, you can find me reading, watching bad reality TV with my dogs, or hunting down my next iced Americano.
